Privacy policy

Information about how EUROTRADEMETAL, spol. s r.o. processes personal data obtained mainly through the contact form, email communication and phone contact.

1. Controller

The controller of personal data is EUROTRADEMETAL, spol. s r.o., with its registered office at Budysinska 38, 831 02 Bratislava, Company ID: 35 707 429.

2. What data we process

Through the contact form, we may process in particular your full name, phone number, email address, message subject and the content of your message, as well as any other data you provide voluntarily.

3. Purpose of processing

We process personal data for the purpose of handling your enquiry, contacting you back, providing requested information and, where there is business interest, also for pre-contractual or contractual communication.

4. Legal basis

The legal basis for processing is primarily taking steps prior to entering into a contract at your request, performance of a contract, the controller's legitimate interest in proper handling of communication and, in justified cases, compliance with legal obligations.

5. Retention period

We retain data for the time necessary to handle your request and subsequent communication, or for the duration of the business relationship, or for the period required by applicable legal regulations.

6. Who may receive the data

Personal data may be disclosed to employees and collaborators of EUROTRADEMETAL, spol. s r.o., as well as to our contractual partners who provide technical, hosting, accounting or legal services, only to the extent necessary.

7. Your rights

You have the right to request access to your personal data, its rectification, erasure or restriction of processing, the right to object to processing and the right to lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Office of the Slovak Republic if you believe that your data is being processed in violation of legal regulations.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding personal data protection, you can contact us at sekretariat@eurotrade.sk or in writing at Budysinska 38, 831 02 Bratislava.
